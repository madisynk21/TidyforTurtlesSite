~Gameplay~

Objective

Save the sea turtles by collecting as much trash as you can without stepping on any innocent baby turtles.

Demo

How to play

Use the WASD keys, arrow keys, or space bar to move Mr. King.

Want to move forward? Press the "D" key or right arrow key.

What about moving backwards? Press the "A" key or left arrow key.

Oh no! There's a turtle in the way! How can Mr. King jump? Press the "W" key, the up arrow key, or the space bar.

How to Score Points

Walk or jump into a piece of trash to collect it. Each piece of trash is worth one point. Be careful, though. If you run into a turtle, you'll lose all of your points and the game.
Pro tip: you have to collect at least 9 pieces of trash in order to win. After all what would be the point of a beach clean up if you didn't pick up a good amount of trash?
